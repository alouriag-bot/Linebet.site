Understanding wagering requirements

Wagering requirements are rules that specify how many times you must bet the bonus before you can withdraw winnings. For example, a 30x wagering requirement on a $100 bonus means you must bet $3,000 before the bonus balance is eligible for withdrawal. Always check these terms carefully to avoid surprises.

Tips to meet wagering requirements

  • Choose bets with medium risk (odds between 1.5 and 2.0) to increase chances of success.
  • Avoid low-odds bets, as some may not count toward requirements.
  • Track your progress in the account dashboard to avoid missed deadlines.
