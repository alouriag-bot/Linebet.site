Linebet Privacy Policy

Complete guide to how Linebet collects, uses, and protects your personal information, including account data, payment details, and communication preferences.

Introduction

At Linebet, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information, comply with legal requirements, and ensure a safe and secure betting experience.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and identification documents.
  • Account information: username, password, account activity, betting history.
  • Payment information: bank account, credit/debit card, e-wallet, and cryptocurrency details.
  • Device and technical data: IP address, browser type, device ID, location data, cookies, and analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage your Linebet account.
  • To process deposits, withdrawals, and bonuses.
  • To communicate updates, promotions, and personalized offers.

Sharing of Information

Linebet does not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information with:

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, secure servers, two-factor authentication (2FA), and regular system audits to protect your personal information.

Cookies and Tracking

Linebet uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze site traffic, and provide personalized offers. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Your Rights

Personal information is used to support responsible gambling, including monitoring betting activity, setting deposit limits, and providing self-exclusion tools.

Contact Us

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact our support team at support@linebet.site.
