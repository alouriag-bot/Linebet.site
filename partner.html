Linebet Partner Program — Affiliates & Sponsorship Opportunities

Complete guide to the Linebet partner program: how to become an affiliate, earn commissions, and take advantage of sponsorship opportunities.

Become a Partner

What is the Linebet Partner Program?

The Linebet Partner Program is designed for affiliates, bloggers, marketers, and businesses to promote Linebet services and earn a commission for every referred player. The program provides a robust tracking system, marketing tools, real-time analytics, and timely payments, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced affiliates.

How to create your affiliate account

  1. Register an affiliate account: Go to the official Linebet Partner Page and click on “Sign Up” to start the registration process.
  2. Fill in personal and business details: Provide your full name, email address, preferred payment method, and tax/business information as required.
  3. Verify your account: Check your email for the confirmation link and submit any KYC documents requested. Verification ensures that commissions can be paid without delays.
  4. Access your affiliate dashboard: After verification, log in to view your unique tracking links, banners, and promotional tools.
  5. Start promoting: Use your referral links on websites, social media, emails, or sponsored events to attract new players.

Commission Structure

Linebet offers flexible commission models to suit different affiliate strategies. The main types include:

1. Revenue Share

Affiliates earn a percentage of the net revenue generated by the players they refer. Typical revenue share rates start at 25% to 40%, depending on the affiliate’s performance and tier level. Higher tiers may offer up to 50% revenue share for top-performing affiliates.

2. CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)

CPA is a fixed payment per new registered and verified player. This model is ideal for affiliates who can drive high-quality traffic without relying on player activity over time.

3. Hybrid Model

Some affiliates can choose a combination of revenue share and CPA. This allows for immediate income from registrations plus ongoing earnings from active player revenue.

Affiliate Tiers & Bonuses

Linebet uses a tiered affiliate program. As your referred player base grows, your commission percentage can increase. Additionally, top affiliates may receive seasonal or performance-based bonuses, exclusive campaigns, and dedicated account managers.

Marketing Tools & Resources

Affiliates have access to a variety of resources to optimize their campaigns:

  • Banners and landing pages in multiple languages.
  • Trackable referral links and QR codes.
  • Performance analytics dashboard for real-time tracking.
  • Content templates, articles, and seasonal promotional materials.

Payment Methods for Affiliates

Linebet supports various payment methods for affiliate commissions, including bank transfers, e-wallets (Skrill, Neteller, etc.), and cryptocurrencies. Payments are processed monthly, and higher-tier affiliates may receive faster or priority payouts.

Advantages of Becoming a Linebet Partner

  • Reliable and timely payments with transparent reporting.
  • High commission potential through revenue share and CPA models.
  • Access to marketing tools and multi-language resources.
  • Dedicated affiliate support and account management.
  • Participation in sponsorship opportunities for broader exposure.

Tips for Successful Affiliate Promotion

  • Focus on high-quality traffic from targeted audiences.
  • Combine multiple channels: websites, blogs, social media, email newsletters.
  • Promote seasonal campaigns and exclusive Linebet offers.
  • Monitor your affiliate dashboard daily and adjust strategies based on performance metrics.
  • Stay updated on Linebet rules, promotions, and marketing guidelines.

Compliance and Terms

Affiliates must comply with Linebet’s terms and conditions, including restrictions on advertising, geolocation limitations, and responsible marketing practices. Non-compliance can lead to commission withholding or account suspension.
